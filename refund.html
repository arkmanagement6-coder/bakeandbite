Refund Policy

At BakeBite, we aim to provide fresh and high-quality bakery products. Please read our refund policy carefully.

1. Refund Eligibility

  • Refund requests must be raised within 24 hours of placing the order.
  • After 24 hours, no refund will be provided under any circumstances.

2. Eligible Cases for Refund

Refunds may be considered in the following situations:

  • Wrong product delivered
  • Damaged or spoiled product
  • Order not delivered

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Requests made after 24 hours
  • Change of mind after order processing
  • Incorrect address provided by customer
  • Failed delivery due to unavailability of recipient

4. Refund Process

  • Once approved, refunds will be processed within 5–7 working days.
  • Refund will be credited to the original payment method.

5. Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing the order.
  • After 24 hours, cancellation is not allowed.
