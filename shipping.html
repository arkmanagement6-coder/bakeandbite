Shipping Policy

At BakeBite, we ensure timely and safe delivery of your orders.

1. Delivery Timeline

Orders are delivered within 24 to 48 hours from order confirmation.

2. Delivery Areas

Delivery is available only in selected serviceable locations.

3. Delivery Charges

  • Delivery charges may vary based on location and order value.
  • Free delivery may be applicable on selected offers.

4. Delivery Attempts

  • If the recipient is unavailable, our delivery team will attempt re-delivery.
  • Additional charges may apply for repeated delivery attempts.

5. Delays

Delivery may be delayed due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Traffic issues
  • Festivals or high demand

We will inform customers in case of delays.

6. Order Tracking

Customers may receive updates via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.

7. Incorrect Address

BakeBite is not responsible for failed deliveries due to incorrect address details.
